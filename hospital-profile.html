Hospital Israelita Albert Einstein is one of the most respected hospitals in Latin America because of their belief that their job is not to treat disease but to care for people. This hospital is noted for its ability to perform highly complex procedures which apply it to meet all the demands of the value chain for the prevention, diagnosis, treatment and rehabilitation.

Hospital Israelita Albert Einstein (HIAE) not only handles your health with respect and attention, it also offers outstanding quality in its customer service, technology, specialties and medical services.

The Hospital was the first institution outside the U.S. to be accredited by Joint Commission International in 1999, when the international program was launched. It was reaccredited in 2003 and again in 2006. This recognition is a seal of exceptionally high quality for the Hospital and for our Diagnostic & Preventive Medicine.

In June 2007, HIAE became Latin America’s first healthcare institution and one of only three worldwide, to have a Stroke Care Center certified by Joint Commission International (JCI). Its interdisciplinary staff are highly trained and equipped with cutting edge technology to treat patients who suffer cerebrovascular accidents (CVA). This infrastructure and continuous improvement of processes are distinctive features that make HIAE a reference in stroke care.

Sociedade Beneficente Israelita Brasileira Albert Einstein (SBIBAE) carries out several initiatives with the intention of stimulating and promoting constant professional development, improving the alliance with its staff and cultivating a work atmosphere where people are satisfied and motivated.
